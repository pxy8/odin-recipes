Gimbap

Description

This recipe is for the Korean food called gimbap. This particular recipe will be a spam and egg gimbap.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed
  • Rice
  • Spam
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Sesame seed oil

Steps

  1. Cook rice in ricecooker
  2. Cook egg and spam with salt
  3. Mix teaspoon of sesame oil into bowl of rice
  4. Put rice thinly on seaweed paper
  5. Put slices of egg and spam in middle of seaweed paper on top of rice
  6. Roll seaweed paper tightly into a cylinder
  7. Slice gimbap roll with knife into individual bite-size pieces
  8. Eat gimbap
